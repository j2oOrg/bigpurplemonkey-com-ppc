Eligibility requirements

Employees of the licensed operator and their immediate family members are not eligible. You must be 18+ and in Alberta at the time of purchase.

Ticket limits and sales window

  • Ticket requests are submitted by email; there is no online checkout on this site.
  • Each ticket costs $10.
  • Only 995 tickets are available.
  • Sales close when tickets sell out or the draw begins.

Draw schedule and venue

The draw takes place March 30, 2026 at Fort Edmonton Park in Edmonton, in line with AGLC requirements.

Prize calculation and payout

One winner receives a prize equal to 50% of total ticket sales. The payout follows the licence terms, is not transferable, and no substitute is provided beyond the authorized amount.

Winner notification and claim period

We post the winner on this website and contact them within five business days using the purchase details. The prize must be claimed within 30 days of notification.

Refunds and cancellations

All ticket purchases are final; refunds and exchanges are not available.

Dispute resolution and oversight

Alberta law and AGLC regulations govern disputes. Decisions of the licensed operator are final, subject to AGLC oversight.

Draw process

Here is a quick look at ticketing, the draw, verification, and payout.

  1. 1

    Ticket sales

    Tickets are $10 each until the 995-ticket cap is reached or sales close at the start of the draw.

  2. 2

    Random draw

    A random draw is held on March 30, 2026 at Fort Edmonton Park in Edmonton.

  3. 3

    Eligibility check

    The selected entry is checked against licence rules and eligibility requirements.

  4. 4

    Prize payout

    After verification, the winner receives 50% of total ticket sales.
