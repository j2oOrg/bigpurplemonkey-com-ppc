Tickets and eligibility

What is the ticket price and how many are available?

Each ticket is $10, and only 995 are issued.

How do I request tickets?

Tickets are requested by email. Send a note to support@bigpurplemonkey.com and we will reply with next steps. There is no online checkout.

Who is eligible to purchase?

You must be 18+ and in Alberta at the time of purchase. Employees of the licensee and their immediate family members are not eligible.

Can I buy more than one ticket?

Yes. You can request multiple tickets, and each ticket is its own entry.

Draw and prize

When and where is the draw conducted?

The draw takes place March 30, 2026 at Fort Edmonton Park in Edmonton, in line with AGLC requirements.

How is the prize calculated?

The winner receives 50% of total ticket sales. The amount changes as sales come in and is paid as set out in the licence.

What are the odds of winning?

At most, odds are 1 in 995 because only 995 tickets are available.

Do I need to attend the draw?

Attendance is not required. We contact the winner and post the result on the site.

Policies and support

How will the winner be contacted?

We contact the winner within five business days using the purchase details. The prize must be claimed within 30 days.

Are ticket purchases refundable?

No. Ticket sales are final and not refundable.

How are proceeds used?

Proceeds fund wildlife conservation and habitat work, including programs like the Pheasant Release Program.

Where can I get more information?

Email support@bigpurplemonkey.com or use the contact section for help.
